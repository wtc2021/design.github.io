Luxury Trends 2019

Ideas & inspiration

The luxury trend combine the highest-quality materials, cutting edge technologies and contemporary appliances to create an astonishing luxurious kitchen. There’s no room that presents a more unique challenge for the designers than the kitchen. Functional luxury is now trending, but don’t be afraid to add some refreshing details that will bring a more elegant look to the kitchen without losing the practicality of the space.

Great lightning for uniqueness

Lighter colors tend to make the space feel larger, while darker ones create a cozy feel. Also the combination of 2 colors, could make the kitchen more open and luxurious.

Carefully chosen lightning will set the mood, and add a unique touch in any living area, especially in a kitchen. Pendant lights are very popular these years, often seen over an kitchen islands.
Be bold in choosing the right one, according to your style, and remember the greatness matters.

Less for more attention

As the minimalist trend is still present, everyone wants their spaces to appear as clean as possible. For those who like it neat, the best way to achieve openness is to eliminate upper cabinets. If you’re building or remodeling this will work beautifully only by adding for example gold details. Rose gold handles are popular, adding more visual warmth than stainless steel.
Also, fancy stoves are must-have if you want that extra luxury in your kitchen.

Gold is black and big is back

Designers recommend rose golds, black golds, bright golds and combining them with high-end appliances and natural materials. Large kitchen island and a beautiful kitchen sink is a great mixture of modern and tradition, that will make your kitchen a classy queen.

If the extravagance is part of your character, but you are not sure how to fit every detail in your kitchen to look great - contact us and schedule a meeting, our designers are here to help you.

We are here to help you with the entire process.
