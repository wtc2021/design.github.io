5 kitchen trends to try in 2020

Trending in 2020

The kitchen is definitely set for a makeover this year. In 2020 a huge, open-concept space with a functional island in the center & big – textural backsplash will dominate. Bright countertops, made of quartz, granite, marble and inevitable open shelving.
If you plan on renovating your kitchen, it will be good to know all about the most recent trends for more inspiration. Choose what will be the most suitable for your space – the possibilities are endless!

#1 Color pops

We are certainly seeing color make a comeback this year. You can now apply it in unexpected places like appliances and hoods, and for more striking effect, at your backsplash or islands. The advice is to use bold tiles in this year the most popular colors (blues, greens, and aquas) to make an impact.

#2 Elegant lighting

Industrial styles are coming back but in more sophisticated forms. Clear pendants as a charming mixture of rustic & industrial and geometric shapes will fit perfectly in any 21st-century decor.

#3 Dark drama vs. pastel tones

2020 is still all about black cabinets. Black is luxe and inviting and combined with textured woods will add rustic, homely charm. Paired with natural materials like marble, they really do make such a gorgeous experience.
Yet, in 2020, we will start to see more colors in kitchens - subtle pastel hues paired with white elements would create a very modern and glamorous kitchen.

#4 Quartz for countertops

Unique styles have become immensely popular this year. When quartz countertops first came into the market, there was a worry of less variety in terms of colors and finishes. But with time, thanks to new technology, we now have a wide range of colors to choose from realistic stone patterns.

#5 Bold backsplash

Large-scale backsplashes are still popular and serve as art and focal points. This is another way to add a pop of color into a kitchen design: you can try an intricate, multi-colored mosaic or maybe a fresh green or aquas is more your thing. Tiles that stray from white are endlessly thrilling.
